A daily AI-driven social deduction game built with **Next.js**.

## рҹ’Ў The Idea
Every 24 hours, a new historical or cultural figure is "incognito." You have **6 questions** to interrogate the AI and uncover their identity.

* **Daily Challenge:** One person, one chance per day.
* **AI Persona:** Interrogate a dynamic AI that reflects the character's real personality without revealing their name.
* **The Dossier:** Successfully identify the target to add their "case file" to your permanent collection.
